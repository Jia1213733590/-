Project Overview

This was a complete website redesign for a tech startup focused on improving user experience and conversion rates. The client needed a modern, responsive website that would showcase their products and services while providing an intuitive user journey.

The design features a clean, minimalist aesthetic with strategic use of color and typography to guide users through the content. Special attention was paid to micro-interactions and animations to create an engaging experience that keeps visitors on the site longer.

Challenge

The main challenge was to balance visual appeal with performance and usability across all devices. The previous website was outdated and had a high bounce rate due to slow loading times and confusing navigation.

Solution

I implemented a mobile-first approach with progressive enhancement to ensure optimal performance on all devices. The navigation was simplified, and clear calls-to-action were strategically placed throughout the site. Performance optimization techniques were applied to achieve fast loading times.

Results

The redesigned website saw a 40% reduction in bounce rate and a 25% increase in conversion rates. Page load times were reduced by 60%, and mobile traffic increased by 35% within the first month after launch.
