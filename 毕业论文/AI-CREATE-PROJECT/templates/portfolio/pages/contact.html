Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about working with me

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started on a project, I'll need information about your business goals, target audience, project requirements, timeline, and budget. I provide a detailed project brief questionnaire that helps me understand your vision. Any existing brand assets or content that you want to incorporate into the project will also be helpful.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A typical website design project takes 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive brand identity project might take 6-8 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide you with a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements and project scope.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I offer various support packages to ensure your project continues to perform well after launch. For websites, this includes maintenance, updates, security monitoring, and technical support. I believe in building long-term relationships with my clients and am committed to your success even after the project is complete.
