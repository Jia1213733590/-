Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A typical website design project takes 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive brand identity project might take 6-8 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide you with a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing options. Project-based pricing is determined after a thorough understanding of your requirements, while hourly rates are available for ongoing work or smaller tasks. I believe in transparent pricing with no hidden costs, and I'll provide a detailed quote before beginning any work.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance for websites?

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to keep your site secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. These packages include regular updates, security monitoring, backup services, and technical support. I can tailor a maintenance plan to suit your specific needs and budget.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I'll need information about your business goals, target audience, project requirements, timeline, and budget. A creative brief or project questionnaire will be provided for you to fill out, which helps me understand your vision. Any existing brand assets or content that you want to incorporate into the project will also be helpful.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide and have established efficient processes for remote collaboration. Using tools like Zoom, Slack, and project management software, we can communicate effectively regardless of location. Regular updates and milestone presentations ensure you're always informed about the project's progress.
